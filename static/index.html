Parsing...

About

This is a small demo of a Perl 6 English parsing module. It matches words against a limited dictionary of parts of speech, and fits the tokens to a context-free grammar. You can read about how it works on my blog.

It currently has support for:

  • Simple sentences (noun phrase followed by verb phrase)
  • Compount sentences (independent clauses separated by "and")

In progress:

  • Imperative sentences
  • Infinitives and verbs as nouns
  • Nouns as adjectives
  • Commas
